Car Avoidance System

The number of autonomous devices in the world is increasing. The next step is to automate cars. To rely on autonomous cars, we have to make them safe. To make cars safe, we need to find a method to avoid collisions. This is our project, make a car avoidance system.
Our final product will advise its user on how to avoid car or stop itself if the user keeps doing the wrong choice. We are aware that it is only the first step of a bigger plan that would make the car avoid another one by itself.
CONCEPT
FEATURES
Control
Control the car using a remote
Detect and compute position
Detect a car in front of the product, and compute its position
Display a graphical help
Display to the user a graphical help to prevent him to create a collision
Take autonomous decisions
Take autonomous decisions to prevent the collision to happen
Detect any error
Detect any error in the car detection process and prevent this error to create a failure
